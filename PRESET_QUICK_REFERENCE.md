## Selection Guide

### Choose by Risk Tolerance
- **Ultra Conservative**: Worst-case planning, stress testing
- **Conservative**: Cautious approach, low expectations
- **Moderate**: Realistic baseline, most common
- **Aggressive**: Best-case planning, growth scenarios

### Choose by Region
- **Western Europe**: High fuel prices, environmental focus
- **Gulf Region**: Low fuel prices, high volume
- **North America**: Balanced market conditions
- **Asia Pacific**: Emerging markets, high growth

### Choose by Station Type
- **Small**: Rural, convenience stores
- **Medium**: Suburban, standard stations
- **Large**: Highway, truck stops
- **Fleet/Industrial**: Private depots, fleets

### Choose by Revenue Model
- **SaaS + Leasing**: Recurring revenue, lower upfront
- **Direct Sales + SaaS**: Hybrid model, upfront + recurring
- **Pure Direct Sales**: Traditional, one-time revenue
- **Debt Financed**: Leverage, tax benefits
